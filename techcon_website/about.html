Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of 50 technology enthusiasts in a local conference center. Over the past decade, we've grown into a global conference attracting over 5,000 attendees from more than 40 countries. Our journey reflects the rapid evolution of technology and our commitment to bringing together the brightest minds in the industry.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to foster innovation, bridge technological gaps, and create a platform where ideas transform into reality. We believe in the power of collaboration, knowledge sharing, and pushing the boundaries of what's possible in technology.

Our core values include:

  • Promoting cutting-edge technological research
  • Encouraging diversity and inclusion in tech
  • Supporting emerging talent and startups
  • Facilitating meaningful connections
